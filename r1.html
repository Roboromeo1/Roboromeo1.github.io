join as reviewer

Star Research is a leading multidisciplinary research journal, with the increased amount of research activities taking place across institutions worldwide; we appreciate the need and role of peer reviewers in ensuring the quality of content published. In this context we appreciate your academic and research activities and solicit your support in strengthening and enhancing the quality of research content being published

We invite you to associate with us as a Reviewer for Star Research. As a Reviewer your name shall be made available to the Reviewer Panel and a letter mentioning your enrollment as a reviewer shall be sent to you. On receipt of articles in your domain of preference, the editorial team would then interact with you for the review of the article.

Please send your expression of interest to editorstarjournal@gmail.com. We shall interact with you to take this forward.
Thank You.
